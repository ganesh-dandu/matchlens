How it Works

Let our AI guide you through a simple 3-step process to evaluate your resume against any job description.

  Paste the Job Description

Copy and paste the job description of the role you’re applying for. This helps our AI understand exactly what the employer is looking for in a candidate.

  Upload Your Resume (PDF)

Upload your resume in PDF format. Our system will scan and extract key details to compare with the job description.

  Click 'Evaluate' to Get Results

Once everything’s ready, click the 'Evaluate' button. You’ll instantly get a detailed analysis of how well your resume matches the job.
